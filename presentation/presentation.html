Home Security Camera; Why?

  1. You leave and nobody else is home.
  2. You have a reckless cat or dog.
  3. Your significant other is unfaithful.
  4. You like to watch what everyone is doing in your house.

There is no such thing as perfect security, only varying levels of insecurity.

Home Security Camera; Issues

  1. Cost; camera, cables, storage, setup.
  2. Complexity; house modifications, camera setup, storage and recording.
  3. Access; local only or remote, access control, protection.
  4. Ownership; yours or by a third-party.

Common solutions for home CCTV

  1. Cost range from 400 to 3000+ euro...
  2. Difficulty in installing and configuring.
  3. In some cases there are no options for customization at all.
  4. You have to use specific types of storage and cables.

The RPI solution

  1. Cheap and scalable; use a simple USB camera or an HD security camera.
    2. <
  2. Easy to setup; hundreds of guides for setting up Raspbian and the camera software.
  3. Highly customisable; based mostly on open software and flexible hardware.
  4. Accessible and secure; access locally or via the Internet, use and configure any type of security measures you prefer.
